# Andrej Karpathy

ML researcher, educator, and builder. Co-founder of OpenAI, former Director of AI at Tesla (Autopilot), creator of the "vibe coding" term, and originator of the [[LLM Wiki Pattern]].

## Key contributions

- **LLM Wiki** — April 2026 GitHub gist describing the personal knowledge wiki architecture using LLM agents
- **Andrej Karpathy's Neural Networks: Zero to Hero** — YouTube series on building LLMs from scratch
- **micrograd, nanoGPT** — educational open-source implementations
- **Tesla Autopilot** — led the vision/perception stack
- **"Vibe coding"** — coined the term for LLM-assisted programming
- **OpenAI co-founder** — one of the original founding team
